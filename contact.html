About Me


After a number of years working in commercial and client-facing roles, I decided to make a significant career change and embarked on the Northcoders Developer Pathway bootcamp.

The programme was a full stack coding bootcamp teaching a practical and industry driven curriculum, with a focus on JavaScript and its frameworks. All code was written with TDD, using best-practice version control with a strong emphasis on pair-programming.

During the final two weeks of the programme, I worked in a team of 5 to deliver an end-to-end application, working with an agile approach to the project, including daily stand-ups, using a kanban board, and presenting the final product during a graduation ceremony. We deliverd this project using all new languages that weren't taught on the course.
